My Portfolio

1: News Glean Final Project

News Glean is an excellent proof of concept of a React based application that strives to use artificial intelligence to show people the news that they want to see. This application allows users to create an account, choose between happy, sad, angry, or exciting news, and then log in to (almost) see news articles that are tailored towards their mood preference.

2: TRAINr Full Stack App

Trainr is a fitness app built using Handlebars, SQL, and Express. The goal of this application is to use a platform where users can record their workouts, then have it displayed in a convienent graph that can be checked later. In this project I managed the SQL database and the graph of the results!
